language: en

Use @auth0/auth0-react for frontend authentication. Wrap the app in Auth0Provider with domain and clientId. Use useAuth0() hook for loginWithRedirect(), logout(), getAccessTokenSilently(). Configure callback URLs for both web (http://localhost:3000/callback) and Electron (staffx://callback) redirect URIs.
